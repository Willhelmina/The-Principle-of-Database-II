1 Resentment kills a fool, and envy slays the simple. 忿怒害死愚妄人，嫉妒杀死痴迷人。
2 Blessed is the man who does not walk in the counsel of the wicked or stand in the way of sinners or sit in the seat of mockers. 不从恶人的计谋，不站罪人的道路，不坐亵慢人的座位。
3 Has no slander on his tongue, who does his neighbor no wrong and casts no slur on his fellowman. 不以舌头谗谤人，不恶待朋友，也不随夥毁谤邻里。
4 A righteous man may have many troubles. 义人多有苦难。
5 Refrain from anger and turn from wrath; do not fret-it leads only to evil. 当止住怒气，离弃忿怒。不要心怀不平，以致作恶。
6 As the deer pants for streams of water, so my soul pants for you. 我的心切慕你，如鹿切慕溪水。
7 Turning your ear to wisdom and applying your heart to understanding 侧耳听智慧，专心求聪明。
8 When the righteous prosper, the city rejoices; when the wicked perish, there are shouts of joy. 义人享福合城喜乐，恶人灭亡人都欢呼。
